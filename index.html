Corporate Identity

FUELS APOSTOLOU SOLE SHAREHOLDER CO. I.K.E. is a prominent enterprise in the Greek retail energy sector. Operating out of Ilion Attikis, our firm manages a strategic network of gasoline service stations designed to meet the demands of modern motorists and commercial fleets.

Under the guidance of Antonios Christos Tsigkelis, we are transitioning from traditional fuel dealers to comprehensive retail destinations, focusing equally on energy provision and consumer convenience.

Industry & Retail

Operating within the broader Retail Trade sector, our services extend beyond the pump. Our stations integrate high-quality fuel dispensing with dedicated convenience stores.

  • Premium Gasoline and Diesel Distribution
  • Automotive Parts and Accessories Retail
  • On-site Convenience & Department Stores
  • Commercial Fleet Invoicing Solutions

Apostolou Rewards & Pay

To eliminate friction at the pump and reward our loyal customers, FUELS APOSTOLOU SOLE SHAREHOLDER CO. I.K.E. maintains a proprietary mobile application on the Google Play Store.

  • Contactless Pump Payments: Pay securely via your Android device without visiting the register.
  • Digital Loyalty Card: Earn points on every liter of fuel or convenience store purchase.
  • Station Locator: Find the nearest Apostolou station, check operating hours, and view live fuel prices.
  • Digital Receipts: Keep track of your fuel expenses and export logs for business accounting.
