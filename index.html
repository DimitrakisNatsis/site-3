Corporate Overview

FUELS APOSTOLOU SOLE SHAREHOLDER CO. I.K.E. is a prominent energy retail enterprise established in Ilion Attikis, Greece. Guided by our Key Principal, Antonios Christos Tsigkelis, we have built a trusted network within the Gasoline Stations and Fuel Dealers sector.

We are more than just a fuel provider. Operating within the broader Retail Trade sector, our service stations act as comprehensive retail hubs, ensuring that both commercial fleet drivers and everyday consumers receive top-tier fuels, automotive accessories, and convenience goods securely and efficiently.

Strategic Energy Retail

Our corporate strategy focuses on the modernization of gasoline service stations. We are actively integrating smart technology to streamline fuel dispensing, B2B fleet invoicing, and customer loyalty programs across our Greek network.

Apostolou Fuel Connect App

To modernize the fueling experience and provide advanced B2B fleet solutions, FUELS APOSTOLOU SOLE SHAREHOLDER CO. I.K.E. has launched a proprietary mobile application published on the Google Play Store.

The Apostolou Fuel Connect app is designed for both individual drivers and corporate fleet managers. It enables contactless payments directly at the pump, digital loyalty points tracking, and real-time fuel consumption reports for logistics companies.

  • Contactless mobile payments directly at the fuel pump.
  • Digital loyalty card integration and reward points tracking.
  • B2B Fleet Portal: manage driver limits and digital invoices.
  • Interactive map locating the nearest operational Apostolou stations.
